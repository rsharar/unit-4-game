THE CRYSTAL CALCULATOR

Directions

  • You will be given a random number at the start of the game

  • There are four crystals to the right. By clicking on a crystal you will add a specific amount of points to your total score.

  • If your score matches the target score, you win! If you go above the target score, you lose.

  • The value of each crystal is hidden from you until you click on it.

  • Each time the game starts, the values of each crystal will change.
